About me

I am Anthony, a neurotherapist and hypnotherapist specializing in emotional detachment, negative emotion management, and healing from trauma and emotional wounds. For over 21 years, I have helped my clients on their journey to emotional freedom and inner healing.

My approach

- Emotional Detachment: I guide you through a process that helps you distance yourself from negative emotions and difficult past experiences. My goal is to help you let go of these burdens, allowing you to fully live in the present.

- Emotion Management: Understanding and managing your emotions is essential for finding clarity and peace of mind. Together, we work on practical techniques to transform your negative emotions into sources of growth and resilience.

- Healing from Trauma: Deep emotional wounds and trauma can have a powerful but invisible impact on your daily life. Through personalized sessions, I assist you in healing these wounds, freeing you from recurring patterns of suffering.

Why choose me?

- A compassionate listener and personalized guidance
- Therapeutic techniques based on the science of emotions and brain function
- A safe space to explore your emotions with confidence

"I truly believe in everyone's ability to transform their lives, and I am passionately committed to helping you overcome your emotional challenges and regain your well-being."
