Web application for therapist

A web application for a neurotherapist and hypnotherapist
specialised in emotional detachment, the management of negative emotions, and the healing of traumas and wounds.

Try the App

Features

Calendly Integration

Streamline appointment scheduling with Calendly integration, making it easy for clients to book sessions directly through the app, eliminating the hassle of back-and-forth emails.

Location Tracking with Google Maps

Help clients easily locate your practice with an embedded Google Maps feature, providing directions and location details for seamless accessibility.

Secure Data Management with SQLite

Ensure data privacy and security with SQLite, allowing efficient storage and management of session notes, client records, and progress tracking within the app.
